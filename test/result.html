*** Four Bedroom Semi-Detached *** Garage and Cellar Space, Perfect for Converting! ***

Purplebricks are pleased to offer to the market this spacious four bedroom semi-detached property. Oozing potential, perhaps convert the garage/cellar as a work from home business or equally a fantastic space for a teen (subject to the necessary planning permissions)

Over four floors this property briefly comprises; open entrance hall with a fantastic bay window, spacious front lounge, second living space which has been extended offering skylights and french doors, great size open plan kitchen/dining area, downstairs WC. To the first floor are four great size bedrooms, and a family bathroom, access to the attic for storage (again a fantastic space to convert subject to the necessary planning permissions). There is garage space and three cellar chambers and an outside patio space.

The property is within close proximity to Heaton Park, within walking distance to the local primary school and ideally situated for local shops, supermarkets and transport links providing easy access to Manchester City Centre and motorway links.
